# ADR-006: Scope Limited to Covenant of Varkaar

Status: Accepted

## Decision

The pre-pilot covers the Covenant of Varkaar lore domain only. No other region, faction, or source document set is in scope until the pre-pilot evaluation criteria are met.

Graduation criteria (all must be satisfied):

- 20–30 lore documents ingested
- ~100–200 reviewed claims stored
- 10 evaluation questions defined and answered using retrieval
- Answers include traceable supporting evidence

## Why

A constrained, well-defined corpus validates the full pipeline end-to-end before scaling. Premature expansion increases complexity without confirming that the core pipeline works.

## Implications

- Source documents, entity extraction, claims, and evaluation questions are all Varkaar-scoped.
- Documents from other regions (Settan Lands, Outer Lands, etc.) are available but must not be loaded until graduation.
- Expanding scope requires satisfying all graduation criteria above, not just some.
