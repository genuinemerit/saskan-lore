# NFR-003: Traceability

## Requirement

Every extracted claim shall be traceable to a specific source chunk and a verbatim source span within that chunk.

## Rationale

Traceability enables verification against source material, contradiction detection across claims, and grounded answering. Without it, the corpus cannot be trusted or audited. See ADR-003 and FR-004.

## Implications

- A claim record without a `chunk_id`, `document_id`, or `source_span` is invalid and must not be loaded.
- Source spans must be verbatim quotes, not paraphrases.
- Human review (NFR-004) must confirm that each source span accurately supports its claim.
- Retrieval results must include source references, not just claim text.
